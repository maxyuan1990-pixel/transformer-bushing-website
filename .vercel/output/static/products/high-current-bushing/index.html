Main Technical Index

Specially designed for connecting the low-voltage side of large power transformers to isolated phase bus ducts, these bushings handle exceptionally high currents with utmost reliability.

Product Advantages

Extreme Current Rating: Engineered for currents up to 40kA with stable thermal and electrical performance.

Solid Insulation Choice: Available in robust RIF or RIP insulation technology.

Superior to Conventional Designs: Unlike traditional porcelain bushings that rely on oil for insulation (prone to uneven field distribution and oil leakage), our solid dielectric design ensures consistent performance and absolute containment.

Custom Design: Built to match specific bushing and busbar interface requirements.

Suitable Service Conditions for Bushings

Ambient Temperature: Suitable for -50 °C to +50 °C.

Installation Angle: Can be installed at any angle.

Ordering Information

When placing an order, please specify rated voltage, rated current, and busbar interface requirements. Custom designs available for specific transformer manufacturers.
