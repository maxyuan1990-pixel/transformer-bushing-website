This bushing provides a direct, reliable connection between overhead lines (or other outdoor equipment) and SF6-insulated metal-enclosed switchgear (GIS).

Product Advantages

True Solid Dielectric Solution: Our RIF or RIP bushings eliminate SF6 gas entirely from the bushing body, solving the endemic problems of gas leakage, pressure monitoring, and costly maintenance.

All-Angle Installation & Low-Temperature Operation: The solid design allows installation at any angle and remains fully functional in extremely low temperatures (below -40°C), where SF6 gas can liquefy and cause insulation failure.

Enhanced Safety: Removes the risk of explosion due to loss of gas pressure or internal flashover.

Custom Design: Adaptable to various GIS interfaces and environmental conditions.

Suitable Service Conditions for Bushings

Altitude: Applicable for installations at or below 2000m above sea level.

Ambient Temperature: Suitable for -50 °C to +50 °C.

Installation Angle: Can be installed at any angle.

Pollution Class: Suitable for heavily polluted areas, Class III or higher.

Ordering Information

When placing an order, please specify:

1. Electrical Parameters: Rated voltage, rated current, rated frequency, external insulation creepage distance, internal insulation clearance, oil-end length, draw-lead length, CT mounting position length.

2. Environmental Parameters: Ambient temperature, altitude, pollution class.

3. Model Designation: The letter "F" in the model number indicates silicone rubber composite sheds. The absence of "F" indicates porcelain sheds.

Note: Custom flange and terminal dimensions available. Direct replacement bushings also supplied.
