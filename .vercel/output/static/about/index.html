We manufacture Resin Impregnated Paper (RIP) and Resin Impregnated Fiber (RIF) bushings covering six core product types:

Resin Impregnated Fiber — solid insulation for demanding environments

RIF dry-type capacitive bushings use a solid capacitor core made of high-performance glass fiber impregnated with epoxy resin and cured at high temperature. Available in draw-lead or draw-rod conductor configurations, with silicone rubber sheds or porcelain external insulation.

RIF technology delivers an oil-free, solid insulation design that is inherently maintenance-free. The compact form factor supports installation at any angle with stable electrical performance and zero risk of combustion or explosion — making it the preferred choice for safety-critical applications.

Resin Impregnated Paper insulation for transformer and GIS interfaces

RIP bushings use resin-impregnated paper as the main insulation system, giving stable electrical performance, compact dimensions, lighter weight and maintenance-free operation for high-voltage transmission and transformation equipment.
